SafeDeal is a trusted escrow intermediary for peer-to-peer commerce. We focus on clarity, fair milestones, and transparent balances so buyers and sellers can transact without taking unnecessary risk.

Problems We Solve

Buyer Protection

Seller Guarantee

Dispute Resolution
